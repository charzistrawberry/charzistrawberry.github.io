Plants

After my first year in college, I had unfortunately fallen into the stereotype of a CS major and often found myself cooped up in my room staring at code for hours and hours trying to decode my homework assignments! This made me begin to appreciate the more "simple" things in life including nature. I began to enjoy my outdoor walks more, looking at the bustling of leaves on trees and observing the scenic greenery! Deeply fascinated by the creations of nature I like to visit my university's greenhouse and look at the unique species of plants that they grow. With my friends and family, I visit botanical gardens and plant stores to admire these living beings some more! Fun fact: I am currently expanding my collection of my favorite plants from the family of asphodelaceae succulents!

Piano

If you asked me what my favorite genre of music was, I would say that I don't have one. I can honestly listen to anything and everything, but classical piano music will have a special place in my heart. Growing up playing on an electric piano, taking lessons every week at my teacher's tiny apartment, and learning about music theory, I believe that music is a universal language that can connect so many people around the world. My favorite memories of playing the piano was when I wrote cello music for my brother to play so that we could perform holidy duets for my family! Fun fact: You may find my on television participating in a talent show!
